zenith_angle__theta_z

public function zenith_angle__theta_z(dLatitude, dDelta, dOmega) result(dTheta_z) bind(c)

Calculate solar zenith angle given latitude, declination, and (optionally) hour angle.

Calculate solar zenith. Solar zenith angle is the angle between a point directly overhead and the center of the sun's disk.

@param[in] rLatitude Latitude of location for which estimate is being made, in RADIANS @param[in] rDelta Solar declination angle, in RADIANS @param[in] rOmega [OPTIONAL] Hour angle, measured at the celestial pole between the observer's meridian and the solar meridian, in RADIANS.

@retval rTheta_z Solar zenith angle for given location and time, in RADIANS

Note

Implementation follows equation 9.67, Jacobson, 2005

Note

Reference: Jacobson, M.Z., 2005, Fundamentals of atmospheric modeling, Second Edition: Cambridge University Press.
