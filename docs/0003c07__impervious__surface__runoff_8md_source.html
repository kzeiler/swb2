1 Impervious surface runoff {#impervious_surface_runoff}
2 =========================================================
3 
4 Runoff from impervious surfaces may be simulated in a more detailed
5 manner by including a gridded dataset defining the proportion of each
6 gridcell that is comprised of impervious materials. Data may be
7 supplied
8 as either a fraction (0.0-1.0) or percentage (0-100%) of either
9 pervious
10 or *im*pervious surface area.
11 
12 Any cell that is assigned an impervious surface fraction or percent
13 that
14 is greater than zero will operate in a fundamentally different way
15 than
16 the original SWB code did; in these cells, mass balance calculations
17 will be performed on an additional "impervious surface storage"
