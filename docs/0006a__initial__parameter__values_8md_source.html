11 Curve Number Aligner
12 --------------------
13 
14 One of the developers of curve number methodology developed a
15 graphical
16 method whereby curve numbers for soil hydrologic group A, C, and D may
17 be estimated from the curve number assigned to soil hydrologic group
18 B.
19 Use of the curve number aligner is encouraged as it will maintain
20 reasonable differences in curve number value between the various soil
21 hydrologic groups for a given landuse.
22 
23 The curve number aligner has been expressed in equation form as given
24 below (Woodward and others, 2003):
25 
26 $CN_A = -60.8 + 1.6083 CN_B$
27 
28 $CN_B = CN_B$
29 
30 $CN_C = 34.0 + 0.6600 CN_B$
31 
32 $CN_D = 47.2 + 0.5283 CN_B$
