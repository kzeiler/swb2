6 Jensen-Haise {#et_jensen_haise}
7 ==================================
8 
9 $E{T_{pot}} = {C_T}\\left( {{T_{mean}} - {T_x}} \\right){R_s}$
10 
11 where:
12 
13 $C_T$ is an air temperature coefficient, and constant for a given area,
14 
15 $T_mean$ is the daily mean air temperature in degrees Fahrenheit,
16 
17 $T_x$ is a constant for a given area,
18 
19 $R_s$ is the daily solar radiation, expressed as equivalent depth of
20 evaporation in inches,
21 
22 $C_T$ is $\\frac{1}{{68 + 13{C_H}}}$
23 
24 $C_H$ is a humidity index given by
25 $\\frac{{37.5\\;{\\text{mm}}\\;{\\text{HG}}}}{{{e_2} - {e_1}}}$, and
26 
27 $e_1$, $e_2$ are the maximum and minimum saturation vapor pressures
28 for
29 the warmest month.
