1 Overview and Capabilities {#overview_and_capabilities}
2 ---------------------------------------------------------
3 
4 SWB capabilities at version 2.0 are largely the same as they were in
5 version 1.0. A simple soil-moisture balance calculation is made on a
6 daily timestep for each active cell within the model domain. Potential
7 recharge (or deep drainage) is considered to occur only when the daily
8 soil-moisture balance generates soil-moisture values that exceed the
9 soil’s field capacity. In this event, the soil-moisture value is reset
10 to the soil’s field capacity, and the excess water is considered to
11 become potential recharge.
