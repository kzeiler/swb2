4 **APWL:** *Accumulated Potential Water Loss.* Represents the running
5 sum
6 of unmet potential evapotranspiration ($P - PE$) over days for which
7 evapotranspiration is greater than precipitation. On days where the
8 precipitation exceeds evapotranspiration, the positive value of $P -
9 PE$
10 is 7added to the previous day's soil moisture to calculate the current
11 day's soil moisture; the soil moisture retention tables
12 (Thornthwaite and Mather, 1957) can then be used to back-calculate a
13 new APWL value for ongoing calculations.
